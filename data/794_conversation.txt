Psychologist: It sounds like you're experiencing some symptoms of depression. How long has this been going on?

Patient: It's been about a month now. I just can't seem to shake this feeling of sadness and emptiness.

Psychologist: Have you noticed any changes in your appetite or interest in activities you used to enjoy?

Patient: Yes, my appetite has definitely changed. I've been eating a lot less lately, and nothing seems to taste good anymore. I hardly have any interest in the things I used to love doing.

Psychologist: There are various treatment options depending on the severity of your depression.
