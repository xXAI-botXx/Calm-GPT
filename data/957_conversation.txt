Patient: Well, I often feel anxious when I have to speak in public or when I'm in crowded places. It feels like everyone is watching and judging me.

Psychologist: Those situations can be challenging for many people. Have you noticed any physical symptoms when you feel anxious?

Patient: Yes, my heart starts racing, I feel sweaty and my hands tremble. Sometimes, I even feel shortness of breath.

Psychologist: These are common physical manifestations of anxiety. It's important to remember that anxiety is a natural response, but we can work on managing it. Have you tried any coping mechanisms or relaxation techniques before?

Patient: I've tried deep breathing exercises, which sometimes help. But I still find myself overwhelmed and unable to relax.

Psychologist: Deep breathing is indeed a helpful technique. I suggest we explore other relaxation techniques together, such as progressive muscle relaxation or mindfulness exercises.
