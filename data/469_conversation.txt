Patient: I've been noticing some changes at work, like budget cuts and downsizing. It's making me doubt my own abilities and feel like I'm on the verge of losing everything.

Patient: I haven't yet, but I suppose it could be helpful to gather more information. It just feels overwhelming right now, you know?
